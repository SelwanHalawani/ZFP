Haram Housing Project - Makkah

A large scale residential development in the heart of Makkah, designed to accommodate pilgrims with modern comfort and optimal access to the Holy Mosque.

Strategically located just minutes from the Grand Mosque to maximize accessibility

Covers over 100,000 square meters, with multiple high-rise residential towers and public facilities.

Designed with modern infrastructure to support high capacity, smooth flow, and safety for residents and visitors.

King Fahd Armed Forces Hospital Expansion - Jeddah

A 300-bed military hospital in Jeddah, with plans for future expansion to 500 beds, covering a total built-up area of 22,800 m². The facility includes specialized outpatient and dental clinics, intensive care (ICU/CCU), cardiac surgery, and micro-surgery units. It also houses royal and VIP suites, alongside a comprehensive range of support facilities such as a 1,200-car multi-story parking structure, an on-site power plant, sewage treatment plant, central laundry, full-service kitchen, medical waste incinerators, pharmacy, and medical records storage. Designed to meet advanced healthcare and operational standards, the hospital supports both clinical excellence and large-scale operational efficiency.
